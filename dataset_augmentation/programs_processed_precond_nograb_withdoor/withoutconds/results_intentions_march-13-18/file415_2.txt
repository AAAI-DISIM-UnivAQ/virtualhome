Wash clothes
Find the basket for dirty clothes. Grab it and move it near the washing machine. Open the door of the washing machine. Open the basket and grab the underwear and put it into the washing machine. Close the basket and washing machine. fine the laundry detergent and squeeze it into the right place of the washing machine. Switch on the washing machine. Put back the detergent.
