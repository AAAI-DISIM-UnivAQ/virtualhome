Brush hair
Part your hair with a comb into two sections. Take a brush, and go through your hair, brushing it from tip to root until there are no tangles.

[Walk] <MIRROR> (1)
[LookAt] <MIRROR> (1)
[Find] <HAIR> (1)
[Brush] <HAIR> (1)
[PutObjBack] <HAIRBRUSH> (1)
