Sweep floor
Remove and rugs or trash cans, etc, out of bathroom.  Sweep everything into a pile.  Sweep into a dustpan, and empty into the trash can, and replace objects.


[Walk] <BATHROOM> (1)
[Find] <BROOM> (1)
[Grab] <BROOM> (1)
[Find] <DUSTPAN> (1)
[Grab] <DUSTPAN> (1)
[Find] <DIRT> (1)
[Grab] <DIRT> (1)
[PutBack] <DIRT> (1) <DUSTPAN> (1)
[Find] <TRASHCAN> (1)
[Grab] <DIRT> (1)
[PutBack] <DIRT> (1) <TRASHCAN> (1)
[PutObjBack] <BROOM> (1)
