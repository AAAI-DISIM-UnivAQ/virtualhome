Get glass of milk
Get an empty glass. Take milk from refrigerator and open it. Pour milk into glass.


[WALK] <dining_room> (1.161)
[WALK] <glass> (1.1000)
[FIND] <glass> (1.1000)
[GRAB] <glass> (1.1000)
[WALK] <fridge> (1.1001)
[OPEN] <fridge> (1.1001)
[FIND] <milk> (1.1002)
[GRAB] <milk> (1.1002)
[POUR] <milk> (1.1002) <glass> (1.1000)
[PUTOBJBACK] <milk> (1.1002)
[CLOSE] <fridge> (1.1001)
