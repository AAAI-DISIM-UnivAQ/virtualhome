Put groceries in Fridge
Collect groceries from kitchen table. Put it inside the fridge.


[FIND] <groceries> (1.1000)
[GRAB] <groceries> (1.1000)
[WALK] <living_room> (1.243)
[WALK] <fridge> (1.1001)
[FIND] <fridge> (1.1001)
[OPEN] <fridge> (1.1001)
[PUTOBJBACK] <groceries> (1.1000)
[CLOSE] <fridge> (1.1001)
