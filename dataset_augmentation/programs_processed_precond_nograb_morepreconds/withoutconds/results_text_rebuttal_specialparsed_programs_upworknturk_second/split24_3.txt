Vacuum
Pull out the vacuum, plug it in and vacuum runner carpet in the entrance hallway.


[Walk] <ENTRANCE HALL> (1)
[Walk] <VACUUM CLEANER> (1)
[Find] <VACUUM CLEANER> (1)
[Grab] <VACUUM CLEANER> (1)
[Find] <ELECTRICAL OUTLET> (1)
[PlugIn] <VACUUM CLEANER> (1)
[SwitchOn] <ELECTRICAL OUTLET> (1)
[Find] <CARPET> (1)
[Vacuum] <CARPET> (1)
[SwitchOff] <ELECTRICAL OUTLET> (1)
[PlugOut] <VACUUM CLEANER> (1)
[PutObjBack] <VACUUM CLEANER> (1)
