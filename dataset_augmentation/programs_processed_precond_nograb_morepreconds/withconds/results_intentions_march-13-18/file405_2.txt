Wash dishes by hand
Find the sponge and the detergent. Put detergent on the sponge. Switch on the faucet. Take the plate from the basin and rub it with sponge. Wash it with the water from the faucet and place it in the dish rack. Switch off the faucet. Put back the detergent and the sponge.
